In my consistency experiment, I observed 0 misses out of 100, which suggests that during my test window replication was fast enough that the newly written user was visible on the other region immediately (or the lag was smaller than my request timing). This still matches eventual consistency since small replication delays can exist, but they were not observable in this particular run.
